Kimchi House History

The first Kimchi House Restaurant opened on October 31, 2016 at Umingan, Pangasinan and it is the first Korean restaurant in Umingan. Five Months later the Korean Restaurant successfully get another branch in Rosales Pangasinan. After many year past by the Korean Restaurant have 200+ branches in the Philipines and over 20 branches in Korea.

Location

The Main location of Kimchi House Restaurant is in Zone 4 Poblacion West Umingan, Pangasinan Philippines. The secondary large branches are in Seoul,Busan, General Santos, Iloilo and Manila. While the small branches is scattered around the Philippines and Korea.
